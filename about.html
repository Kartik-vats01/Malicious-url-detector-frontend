Our Story

SecureLink was founded in 2023 by a group of cybersecurity experts and machine learning engineers who witnessed the devastating impact of phishing attacks on individuals and organizations worldwide.

Frustrated by the limitations of existing solutions, our founding team decided to create a better approach. They spent months researching, developing, and training advanced ML models to identify phishing URLs with unprecedented accuracy.

Today, SecureLink has grown into a trusted platform used by thousands of users across the globe. Our hybrid machine learning approach combines deep learning, signature-based detection, and heuristic analysis to provide real-time protection against evolving phishing threats.

We remain committed to our mission: making the internet safer, one scan at a time.
